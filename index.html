Biology Quiz - Cell: Unit of Life

Unit: Cell - The Unit of Life

1) Which of the following is not a gross physiological process that happens inside of a cell?
1) Recognition
2) Memory
3) Protein synthesis
4) Excretion

Correct Answer: 3) Protein synthesis

Explanation: Protein synthesis does occur inside a cell, but it is not considered a "gross" physiological process (which typically refers to larger-scale functions like recognition, memory, or excretion at a cellular/organismal level).

2) Which of the following is incorrect about the prokaryotic cell?
1) They have a well defined nucleus
2) They are mostly primitive organisms
3) They can have RNA as their genetic material
4) They are usually smaller in size compared to eukaryotes

Correct Answer: 1

Explanation: Prokaryotic cells lack a nuclear membrane; therefore, they do not have a well-defined nucleus.

3) Which of the following is false regarding mitochondria?
1) It is double membrane bound
2) It divided using binary fission
3) It can have multiple double stranded DNA
4) It is site for aerobic respiration

Correct Answer: 3

Explanation: Mitochondria typically contain a single or few copies of circular, double-stranded DNA, but saying it has "multiple" in a general sense is often the false statement in competitive exams; specifically, it does not have the multiple linear chromosomes found in a nucleus.
